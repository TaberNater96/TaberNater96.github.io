Driven by an insatiable curiosity about the universe and an unwavering commitment to solving complex problems, I am Elijah Taber — a data scientist, physicist, and Army veteran who thrives at the intersection of scientific discovery and technological innovation.

My academic journey reflects my diverse passion for understanding the world around us. With a Bachelor's degree in Physics complemented by a minor in Geology, I've explored the fundamental laws that govern everything from quantum mechanics to planetary formation. This foundation was further strengthened by earning a Bachelor's degree in Data Science with a minor in Computer Information Systems, equipping me with the computational tools to tackle humanity's most pressing challenges.

What sets me apart is my relentless pursuit of knowledge and problem-solving. Whether I'm analyzing atmospheric data to combat climate change, diving deep into quantum mechanical principles, or pondering the origins of the universe, I approach each challenge with the same methodical rigor and unbridled enthusiasm. My expertise spans the entire technology stack — from crafting intuitive front-end experiences to architecting robust backend systems, and from building efficient data pipelines to developing sophisticated machine learning models.

I code seven days a week, not because I have to, but because I genuinely love the process of transforming complex ideas into elegant solutions. This dedication stems from my belief that technology and science are humanity's greatest tools for understanding and improving our world. Every line of code I write, every dataset I analyze, and every model I build brings us one step closer to answering the profound questions that have captivated scientists for generations.

My military background has instilled in me the discipline, leadership, and perseverance necessary to tackle seemingly impossible problems. Combined with my scientific training and technical expertise, I bring a unique perspective to every project — one that bridges theoretical understanding with practical implementation, always keeping the bigger picture in mind.
